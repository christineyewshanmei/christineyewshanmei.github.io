Once upon a time...

In a small village at the edge of a quiet forest, there lived a girl named Shoty. Shoty loved adventures more than anything. Every morning, while others stayed home, she explored hidden paths and collected unusual things—blue leaves, shiny stones, and stories from travelers. One day, while walking deeper into the forest than ever before, she found a tiny wooden door in the trunk of a giant tree. Curious, Shoty knocked. Knock. Knock. Knock. Door slowly opened. Inside lived a tiny fox wearing round glasses. “Welcome,” said the fox. “I've been waiting for someone brave.”
Shoty blinked. “For me?” The fox nodded and handed her a small golden seed. “This seed only grows if someone shares kindness.” Shoty didn't understand, but she took the seed home. Over the next few days, she helped her neighbors—carrying baskets, reading stories to children, and sharing food. Every kind thing she did made the seed glow brighter. Then one morning— A huge golden tree appeared in the center of the village. Its branches gave fruit that never ran out, and its leaves lit the village at night. Everyone asked Shoty how she did it. She smiled and said: “Some treasures don't grow from magic. They grow from kindness.” And from that day on, whenever someone did something kind, a golden leaf appeared on the tree.
