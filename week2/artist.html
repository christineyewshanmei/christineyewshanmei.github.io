Introduction

Juhoon (birth name: Kim Ju-hoon) is from Korea and is one of the members of CORTIS. Besides speaking Korean, he can also speak English. He participated in the choreography for CORTIS “GO!” and is known as the chill member of the group. He is also musically talented and can play the piano, flute, and guitar.
